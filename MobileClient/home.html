About Us

Student Tech is a growing business whose main goal is to make technology more affordable to students and education institutes in Australia and around the world by selling high-end technology at reasonable prices.

Students can get a 15% discount by providing their valid student ID

Support our Charity


STFoundation.org

STFoundation is a not-for-profit organisation that uses its proceeds to provide shelter to the homeless, relieve hunger, provide emergency relief, and issue scholarships to those who can't afford an education.
